About Me

Hello! I’m Ashwathy, and I’m passionate about creating efficient, secure, and scalable systems that power the cloud. My interest in cloud computing and automation began during my time as a systems engineer when I automated enterprise device requests processes to Service Now— discovering the joy of turning complex challenges into streamlined solutions.

Fast forward to today, I’ve had the privilege of working at banking and huge corporations, contributing by automating Python scripts to validate HDFS data and building CI/CD pipelines with GCP tools like Cloud Build and Kubernetes. My focus these days is on leveraging DevOps practices and cloud technologies to create infrastructure that’s as secure as it is scalable.

In my free time, I enjoy experimenting with new tools and technologies, like building monitoring dashboards with Prometheus and Grafana or diving into infrastructure automation with Terraform. I’m always learning and looking for ways to contribute to innovative projects that push boundaries!
